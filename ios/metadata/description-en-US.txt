Sticks is the fastest way to keep score, know your number, and play your group's games — all in one round.

LIVE GPS, EVERY HOLE
See real distances to the front, center, and back of every green, plus carries to hazards and bunkers — updated as you walk. Clear satellite hole views so you can pick your line.

SCORING THAT KEEPS UP
Tap in scores for the whole group in seconds. Net and gross, automatic handicap strokes, and a running leaderboard that updates on every hole. Start a round solo or with up to your whole foursome.

YOUR HANDICAP, HANDLED
Sticks tracks your scoring history and target index so you always know where you stand — no spreadsheet required.

THE GAMES YOU ACTUALLY PLAY
Run the side games your group loves — Nassau, Skins, Wolf, Snake, Bingo-Bango-Bongo, Sixes, Stableford, Match play and more. Sticks tallies the standings automatically so there's no arguing on the 18th.

Note: Sticks only keeps score. Any dollar amounts are just to help tally who owes whom — no money is ever collected, held, or paid through the app. Players settle up on their own.

A LIVE PREDICTION MARKET (JUST FOR FUN)
Every player gets live win odds that blend a model, the crowd, and the live score — repricing on every shot. Tap Place your pick to back who you think takes it. It's play-money and social — no real money, ever.

TOURNAMENTS & GROUPS
Set up a group, invite your friends, and run a tournament — from a weekend Nassau to a full best-ball event with a live leaderboard. Follow other players to see their rounds in your feed.

BUILT FOR THE COURSE
- Apple Watch: keep your round alive on your wrist
- Fast, glanceable scoring designed for pace of play
- Works for casual rounds, leagues, and tournaments
